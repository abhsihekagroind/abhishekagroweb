About Abhishek Agro

18+ years of dedication · Rooted in quality · Growing with Nepal

Our Story

From a small trading post in Bhairahawa to a nationally trusted rice supplier.

Abhishek Agro Industries was founded in 2005 with a simple vision: to provide every Nepali family with access to pure, high-quality rice at fair prices. What started as a modest family business in Siddharthanagar Municipality-04, Bhairahawa, has grown into one of Nepal's most respected rice trading companies.

Over the past 18+ years, we have built strong relationships with farmers across the terai region, ensuring they receive fair compensation for their harvest. Our state-of-the-art milling facility and rigorous quality checks guarantee that only the finest grains reach our customers – from household kitchens to five-star hotels.

Today, we proudly serve over 50,000 happy customers across all 77 districts of Nepal. Our product range includes premium varieties like SONAM (Sabha Jeera Masino), DAYA BHOG (Katarni & Jeera), and SWAD BHOG (Silky, Mota, and Premium).

Our Mission

To deliver superior quality rice products while supporting local agriculture, maintaining ethical business practices, and ensuring customer delight.

Our Vision

To revolutionize Nepal's rice industry by setting new benchmarks in quality, sustainability, and innovation – becoming the most trusted rice brand nationwide.
