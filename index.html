About Abhishek Agro

With a legacy of 18+ years, we are Nepal's leading rice trading company committed to quality, trust, and customer satisfaction.

Established in 2005 in Siddharthanagar Municipality, Bhairahawa, Abhishek Agro Industries has grown to become one of Nepal's most trusted rice suppliers. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide every Nepali household with access to high-quality, nutritious rice at fair prices.

We work directly with local farmers across Nepal's fertile regions, ensuring fair trade practices while maintaining rigorous quality standards. Our state-of-the-art processing facilities and quality control measures guarantee that only the finest rice reaches your table.

Our Mission

To deliver superior quality rice products while supporting local agriculture and maintaining ethical business practices.

Our Vision

To revolutionize Nepal's rice industry by setting new standards in quality, sustainability, and customer service.
