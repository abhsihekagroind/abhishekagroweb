Our Quality Process

From the paddy field to your kitchen – every step reflects our commitment to purity

Seed to Serving Bowl

We follow a rigorous, transparent process to ensure every grain meets the highest standard.

1. Farm selection & sowing

We partner with experienced farmers from the fertile terai regions. Soil testing, seed quality checks, and sustainable practices begin here.

2. Natural cultivation

Our farmers use traditional knowledge combined with modern irrigation. No excessive chemicals – just the right balance of water and organic nutrients.

3. Harvesting at peak

We harvest at precise maturity when the grains are richest in aroma and texture. Mostly hand-picked to preserve integrity.

4. Drying & storage

Sun-dried on large clean yards, then stored in aerated silos. Moisture content is strictly controlled to prevent spoilage.

5. Milling & polishing

State-of-the-art mills remove husk gently. Depending on variety (silky, jeera, katarni) we apply just the right polishing for shine and nutrition.

6. 7-point quality check

Every batch undergoes lab tests for purity, broken ratio, aroma, and taste. Only after passing 7 checks it’s approved for packing.

7. Hygienic packaging

Rice is packed in food-grade bags (25kg, 30kg, 50kg) with proper sealing. Each bag carries batch number and date.

8. Nationwide delivery

From our warehouse in Bhairahawa, we dispatch to all 77 districts. Our logistics partners ensure timely, safe delivery.

ISO & FSSAI certified

Our facilities meet international food safety standards. Regular audits guarantee consistent quality.

In-house lab

We test for pesticide residue, moisture, and aroma. Only the cleanest rice moves forward.

Farmer partnership

We train and support local farmers, ensuring fair price and sustainable agriculture.

18+ years expertise

Decades of refinement have shaped our process. Every year we improve.
